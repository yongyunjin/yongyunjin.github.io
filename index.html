Yongyun Jin is currently a graduate student in Smart Building at Tianjin University, working closely with Prof. Juanli Guo and Prof. Jianhua Zhao of the Tianjin Key Laboratory of Architectural Physics and Environmental Technology. During his graduate study, he has participated in multiple research projects of different topics:

  • National Key R & D Program of China [No. 2019YFD1101004]
  • Tianjin Natural Science Foundation [No. 18JCQNJC08200]
  • Opening Fund of State Key Laboratory of Green Building in Western China [No. LSKF201904]
  • State Grid Corporation of China [No. KJ20-1-49]

So far, he has published 6 journal papers, 1 invention patent, and won multiple international competitions.He is expected to graduate in June 2023 and is looking for a Ph.D. position to conduct deeper research.
